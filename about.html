What we do

Digital products that people love to use

A product vision needs a full-service partner who can assist with its conception, production, and scaling. At Humane, we prioritize great products over rigorous procedures, which is why we’ve perfected our iterative, human-centered approach to product design and delivery. We assist in the definition of adaptable processes that not only provide results for our customers' product teams and companies, but also position them as long-term competitors in their industries.

Enterprise software that doesn't suck

We can quickly develop and bring to market products and customer experiences that people enjoy because to the efficient operating models DesignOps and DevOps, which support our design thinking approach and agile skills. To achieve good interaction design, our UX design, service design, product design, and product development teams test products frequently and early with actual users.

Websites that tell your brand's story

From Slack to Facebook, we team up with marketing departments of large companies and startups to deliver award‑winning websites.

Customer Experience Design & Strategy

With our expertise in org design and activation, we help organizations apply their brand and experience principles internally to create a single, holistic experience strategy that adds value for customers, employees and the business itself.

Service Design

By visualizing your future customer experience, articulating the vision, defining the service proposition, and assisting with development and launch, we help create new services. We have specific expertise in developing and launching services for firms that focus on products.
